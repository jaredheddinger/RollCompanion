name Silence
source PHB.275
school illusion
level 2
ritual 1
casttime 1 action
range 120 feet
components V, S
duration Concentration, up to 10 minutes
text For the duration, no sound can be created within or pass through a 20-foot-radius sphere centered on a point you choose within range. Any creature or object entirely inside the sphere is immune to thunder damage, and creatures are deafened while entirely inside it.
